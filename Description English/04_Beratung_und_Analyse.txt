# Consulting and Analysis

- Creditreform provides expertise in enriching master data, analyzing customer data, and determining customized credit limits.
- Creditreform specializes in data-driven evaluations and possesses extensive knowledge due to the breadth and depth of information in its database.
- Creditreform can access raw data and consolidate it into meaningful analyses, such as market potential analyses or comparisons of customer payment behaviors.
- These analyses enable companies to define and standardize individual credit decisions.
- Creditreform supports the identification of customers and the enhancement of internal information with creditworthiness indicators.
