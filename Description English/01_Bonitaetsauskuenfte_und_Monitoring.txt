# Purchasing Credit Reports from Abroad

## Domestic Credit Reports
(https://www.creditreform.ch/loesungen/auskuenfte-und-monitoring)
- **Creditreform provides credit reports** to create transparency in business relationships and minimize risks.
- These reports help companies **assess the ability of business partners** to meet their payment obligations.
- Creditreform **evaluates credit-relevant information** and presents the results in the form of partial scores, risk classes, credit bands, or credit traffic lights.
- In addition to creditworthiness, **the reports contain further information**, providing a comprehensive picture of the customer.
- Creditreform offers **reports on companies and private individuals** both domestically and internationally.
- With credit and business reports, **companies can know in advance** who they are dealing with and assess the risk of loss.
- Creditreform offers **various reporting products** such as CrediPLUS, CrediRISK, CrediCHECK, and CrediDATA.
- For **reports outside Switzerland and Liechtenstein**, additional international surcharges usually apply, and countries are grouped into country categories.

## International Credit Reports, International Business Information
(https://www.creditreform.ch/fileadmin/user_upload/central_files/_documents/01_loesungen/11_auskunft_monitoring/05_Flyer_International_DE.pdf)

**General Objectives and Benefits**  
Creditreform's International Business Information supports companies in **minimizing risks in international business** by providing detailed credit information on foreign business partners. This is particularly important, as international business not only offers growth opportunities but also involves **increased entrepreneurial risks**.

**Areas of Application**  
- **Assessment of creditworthiness and risk classification** of international customers and suppliers  
- Support for informed credit decisions for **business volumes of any size** as well as project business  
- **Determination and adjustment** of credit limits for existing and new customers  
- **Review, verification, and adjustment** of payment agreements  
- Protection in **contract conclusions and prevention** of fraud  
- Availability of **company master data and information on authorized signatories**

**Features and Services**  
- **International availability** through a network of market-leading partners, including Creditreform national subsidiaries and other partners worldwide  
- Information is available in a **standardized and multilingual format** (German, French, English, soon also Italian)  
- **IT integration** is possible via standardized interfaces as well as individual systems  
- Availability via the **online platform "CrediWEB"**  
- Possibility of **individual research** in addition to standard reports  
- For numerous countries, **automatic monitoring** of credit information is possible

**Product Offerings and Country Groups**  
- There are **four tiered information products**, tailored to respective risk requirements  
- International surcharges are **added to the base price of the respective reporting products**  
- The **level of detail in reports varies by country**  
- Information typically includes **detailed data on creditworthiness, financial circumstances, and the business environment** of the respective international partner

**Advantages at a Glance**  
- **Objective and reliable basis** for evaluating international business partners  
- **Comprehensive, well-founded, and exclusive information** reduces uncertainty in decision-making  
- **Transparency and timeliness** through standardized formats and automatic updates  
- **Global presence** through local market leaders on-site

**Further Links**  
- [Creditreform International](http://www.creditreform.com/)  
- [BigNet Alliance](http://www.bignetalliance.com/)

Thus, Creditreform's International Business Information provides an **important tool for securing** and successfully managing **international business relationships**.

# Monitoring

## Monitoring Switzerland
https://www.creditreform.ch/fileadmin/user_upload/central_files/_documents/01_loesungen/11_auskunft_monitoring/01_Prospekt_Auskuenfte_Monitoing_DE.pdf
- Creditreform offers monitoring services to keep companies informed about current changes regarding their customers.
- Systematic monitoring continuously provides up-to-date data, and companies can decide which information is relevant to them.
- Notifications can be integrated into companies' own applications.
- Creditreform monitors credit-relevant changes as well as master data.
- Monitoring products differ in the depth of information provided.
- Signal monitoring is included with CrediRISK and CrediCHECK.
- Monitoring management in CrediWEB allows management and retrieval of monitoring activities.

## International Monitoring

- Strategic partners or key customers can also be monitored in the most important European countries.

## ComplianceCHECK
https://www.creditreform.ch/loesungen/know-your-customer  
https://www.creditreform.ch/fileadmin/user_upload/central_files/_documents/01_loesungen/11_auskunft_monitoring/04_Flyer_ComplianceCHECK_DE.pdf  

- Creditreform supports financial intermediaries in complying with the Anti-Money Laundering Ordinance (GwV).  
- ComplianceCHECK includes verification of the identity of beneficial owners, politically exposed persons (PEP), former PEPs, as well as checks against sanction lists, watchlists, and blacklists.  
- ComplianceCHECK reports can be obtained either via CrediWEB for individual cases or via the CrediCONNECT interface.

## Consulting and Analysis

- Creditreform provides expertise in enriching master data, analyzing customer data, and determining customized credit limits.
- Creditreform specializes in data-driven evaluations and possesses extensive knowledge due to the breadth and depth of information in its database.
- Creditreform can access raw data and consolidate it into meaningful analyses, such as market potential analyses or comparisons of customer payment behaviors.
- These analyses enable companies to define and standardize individual credit decisions.
- Creditreform supports the identification of customers and the enhancement of internal information with creditworthiness indicators.
